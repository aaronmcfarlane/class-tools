⚠️ Protecting Your Hearing
Sounds above 85 dB can damage your hearing if you're exposed for too long. Sounds above 130 dB can damage it instantly. You can protect yourself by wearing earplugs or earmuffs, and by keeping the volume on your headphones below 60%.
